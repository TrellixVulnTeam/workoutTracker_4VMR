What is this?
This page currently hosts one of my projects which I am toying around with - a workout tracker.
I created this workout tracker due to the workout trackers on the market not fullfilling my needs. My workout tracker allows me to create exercises, workouts and workoutplans and to track workouts and even workoutplans. The workoutplans and its tracking handle the automatic weight and rep progression for me to maximize the effect of my workouts. Additionally they automatically adjust the workouts in deload weeks. This is what most workout trackers on the market are missing - workoutplans with automatic progression and deloads.
How does it work?
The workout tracker is a Node.js application written in Angular and hosted on Heroku. The background is handled by a PHP-API (also hosted on Heroku) which handles the requests to a database (Heroku Postgres) storing all the data needed.
